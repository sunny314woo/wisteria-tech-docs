## What to Look for in a ChatGPT Outline Extension

Before comparing specific tools, it helps to know what matters:

**Automatic outline generation.** The extension should extract all messages automatically as you load a conversation. No manual setup required.

**Clean sidebar design.** The outline should be easy to scan, with visual hierarchy between user messages and assistant responses.

**Bookmark support.** The ability to mark messages as important and see them highlighted in the outline.

**Export capability.** The best extensions combine outline navigation with Markdown export, so you do not need two separate tools.

**Cross-platform support.** If you use both ChatGPT and Gemini, an extension that works on both platforms is more valuable than one that works on only one.

**Privacy.** The extension should process all data locally in your browser and not send your conversations to external servers.

## FAQ

### Is a ChatGPT outline extension safe to use?

Reputable extensions process all data locally in your browser. ChatGPT Gemini Outline & Export does not transmit your conversation data to any external server, making it safe for sensitive work.

### Do outline extensions work with GPT-4 and GPT-4o?

Yes. Outline extensions work at the interface level and are compatible with all ChatGPT models.

### Can I use an outline extension with the new ChatGPT interface?

Yes. Extensions adapt to ChatGPT's interface changes. Regular updates ensure compatibility.

### Do I need to be online for the outline extension to work?

Yes. The extension works within the ChatGPT web app, which requires an internet connection. However, your exported Markdown files are available offline.

### Will an outline extension slow down ChatGPT?

Well-designed extensions have minimal performance impact. They run in the browser background and only activate on ChatGPT and Gemini pages.
