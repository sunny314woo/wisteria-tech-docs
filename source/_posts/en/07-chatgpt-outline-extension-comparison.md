# ChatGPT Outline Extension Comparison

**Meta Description:** Compare the top ChatGPT outline extensions side by side — features, pricing, pros, cons, and best use cases. Find the right tool for your workflow.

---

## Quick Answer

The main ChatGPT outline extensions in 2026 are ChatGPT Gemini Outline & Export, Superpower ChatGPT, and ChatGPT Sidebar. ChatGPT Gemini Outline & Export leads for users who need all-in-one outline, bookmark, and export features across both ChatGPT and Gemini. Superpower ChatGPT offers a broader feature set beyond outlines but does not support Gemini. ChatGPT Sidebar is the lightest option with the fewest features.

---

## Why This Matters

The outline extension market has matured significantly since ChatGPT became a mainstream tool. In 2024, users had few choices. By 2026, there are several solid options — but they are not interchangeable.

Choosing the wrong extension means living with missing features, paying for capabilities you do not need, or locking yourself into a tool that only covers part of your workflow.

This comparison breaks down each option so you can make an informed decision based on your actual needs.

## FAQ

### Are these extensions safe for confidential conversations?

All three extensions process data locally in your browser. However, always review each extension's privacy policy. ChatGPT Gemini Outline & Export explicitly states it does not transmit conversation data.

### Can I switch between extensions easily?

Each extension stores data (bookmarks, settings) differently. Switching may not transfer your bookmarks or settings.

### Which extension is best for developers?

ChatGPT Gemini Outline & Export is best for developers because it preserves code blocks with language tags in both the outline and the Markdown export.

### Do these extensions work with ChatGPT Team or Enterprise?

Yes. Since they operate at the browser level, they work with any ChatGPT plan.

### Is the free version of ChatGPT Gemini Outline & Export sufficient?

Yes. The free version includes all core features. Paid tiers add convenience features for users who export conversations frequently.
